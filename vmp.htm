WORD IS ABOUT TO BE REAL OUT THERE


Be the part of the movement where everything is possible we known as the movement that makes something out of nothing.s Visible music productions (VMP) are a Movement started with the idea of just trying to record Hip Hop music in the Township of Zwelethemba,
Worcester just outside Cape Town. The movement was started in the year 2010 by Ntabethemba Mabetshe, after struggling so much to find a place to record He then decide to get a Computer and hustle some software's like Cubase, FL Studio. Even though He struggled to make beats at first at the end He finally figure it out how to lay down patterns, on FL Studio and the right Tempos for hip hop beats that was all He needed to know to get Him started with the recordings.
Soon after the beginning of the recordings Maneza started distributing His Music amongst his friends and people he knew songs started to spread in Zwelethemba with Bluetooth sharing and the other ways, More Artists started to get interested in working with Maneza F8 at VMP
Artists like Buso Baxa, Gladwell, Simamnkele, Ricardo, Sandile Xaba, Olwethu Ludidi, Vuyani Mini, Which the call themselves Visible Squad, they started by recording at another young dude's home studio called Sight records back in those days, they came with a Hit single titled Izwelethemba Neziyobisi (drugs in Zwelethemba). The track received so much love in a way that it spreaded in some other nearby townships and Visible squard was then popular as well as Maneza F8 who was then MC_MANEZA, there was no way you can talk about hip hop in Worcester, Zwelethemba without mentioning Mc_maneza.
